# Implementation Plan: Perrault-Inspired Fairy Tale Draft

## Objective
To draft a 150-word story based on a Charles Perrault fairy tale, adhering to the narrative conventions established in the 2026-02-19 ADR.

## Tasks

### 1. Source Material Selection
- Identify a primary tale from *Histoires ou contes du temps passé* (1697).
- Candidate: *La Barbe bleue* (Bluebeard) due to its strong cautionary themes and dark atmosphere.

### 2. Narrative Drafting
- **Opening**: Utilize the traditional "Il était une fois" (Once upon a time) structure.
- **Word Count**: Maintain a concise 150-word limit for the core narrative.
- **Tone**: Emphasize the darker, 17th-century French cautionary elements rather than modern sanitized versions.

### 3. Formal Coda
- Compose a rhymed or structured moralizing coda (*Moralité*) to conclude the tale, as is standard in Perrault's literary style.

### 4. Verification and Archiving
- Ensure the final draft is saved to the `.vault/stories/` directory.
- Verify that the word count and narrative markers align with the ADR requirements.
