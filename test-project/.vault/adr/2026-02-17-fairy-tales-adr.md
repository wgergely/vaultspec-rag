# ADR: Adoption of Perrault's 1697 Framework

## Decision
We will adopt Charles Perrault’s 1697 collection, *Histoires ou contes du temps passé*, as the foundational architectural model for our literary research database.

## Context
Research indicates Perrault’s work uniquely bridged oral folklore and courtly literature, establishing the "once upon a time" convention and explicit *moralités*. 

## Consequences
This framework enables a systematic comparison between peasant motifs and high-literary structures. All future entries must categorize stories based on their transition from oral tradition to courtly adaptation, ensuring a consistent analytical baseline for our study of French folklore.
