# ADR: Standardizing Fairy Tale Narrative Conventions

## Decision
We will standardize the vault's fairy tale research and creative drafts based on the literary conventions established by Charles Perrault in his 1697 collection, *Histoires ou contes du temps passé*.

## Context
Research confirms Perrault as the formalizer of the literary fairy tale genre, introducing foundational structures for Western folklore.

## Consequences
New story drafts will incorporate narrative markers such as "once upon a time" and explicit moralizing codas. This ensures historical consistency with 17th-century French traditions while respecting the darker, cautionary origins of these adapted folk tales.
